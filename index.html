Welcome to LiftTrack

Here's a quick tour of what you can do:

📊
Dashboard
Your personalized home — track activity, body weight, PRs, and more. Tap "Customize" to drag and reorder panels.
💪
Log Workout
Log lifts, runs, or full session templates. Tap Start Live for a guided, set-by-set workout.
📋
Library
Browse every exercise with demo silhouettes and PRs. Build stretch routines with the built-in timer.
🍎
Nutrition
Log calories & protein, search packaged foods, save quick presets, and set body weight goals with ETAs.
📅
History
Review every session, repeat past workouts, and watch your progress compound over time.
⚙️
Settings
Pick your color scheme or edit your profile from the gear icon in the top right.
Show / Hide Panels
Toggle panels on or off. Use "Edit Layout" on the dashboard to drag-and-drop the order.
CSV Format

Your CSV file should have the following columns. The header row is optional.

exercise,sets,reps,weight
Barbell Bench Press,4,8,185
Incline Dumbbell Press,3,10,65
Lateral Raise,3,15,20
• exercise — must match an exercise name in the app
• sets — number of sets (optional, defaults to 3)
• reps — reps per set
• weight — weight in lbs (use 0 for bodyweight)
